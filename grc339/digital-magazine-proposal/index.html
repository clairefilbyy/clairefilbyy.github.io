The Typographic Almanac

Welcome to The Typographic Almanac! We highlight typefaces created by indie designers who bring fresh ideas and playful energy to the world of typography. Whether you’re looking for something bold, quirky, elegant, or entirely unexpected, you’ll find fonts here that make your work stand out. Take a look around and support the creative minds behind the letters.
